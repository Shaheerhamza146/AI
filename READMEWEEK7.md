Lab 7: Information Extraction & NER Pipeline


Project Overview

This project transforms unstructured document images (invoices and receipts) into structured JSON data. By combining Optical Character Recognition (OCR), Regular Expressions (Regex), and Named Entity Recognition (NER), the system automatically identifies and extracts key business information such as dates, currency amounts, and organization names.
